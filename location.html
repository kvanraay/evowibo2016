Location

Fort Worden is located in Port Townsend, Washington, on the tip of the Olympic Peninsula. It’s an easy two-and-a-half-hour trip from Seattle and three hours from Vancouver, B.C., but make sure to take in the sights along the way. If you’re coming from the North, check out La Conner or Whidbey Island, or even take a side trip to Mt. Baker National Forest. Coming from the south, hop a ferry to Bainbridge Island, and experience the scenic route through coastal towns such as Poulsbo and Port Gamble. Whichever way you get here, make your journey as memorable as your visit here. 200 Battery Way, Port Townsend, WA 98368
