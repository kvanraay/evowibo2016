Registration

IMPORTANT: Registration must be completed in two steps. First, please fill out the registration form, then visit PayPal to submit your payment. Your registration will not be complete until BOTH have been done. Registration deadline is March 1st.

Students can save $30 off registration through membership with the American Society for Naturalists. (A net savings of ~$8.50 after accounting for membership fees).

Note: We are striving to make this meeting accessible to all evolutionary biologists in the Pacific Northwest. We are working to provide childcare as well as facilities for nursing moms. Wheelchair accessible housing is available.

We are at capacity for EVO-WIBO 2016 as of 4pm PST 29 Feb 2016. If you are still interested in attending, please register but do not pay until you have received an e-mail from us. If you ahve registered but have not paid, please pay ASAP or you will lose your spot.
