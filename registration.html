Registration

IMPORTANT: Registration must be completed in two steps. First, please fill out the registration form, then visit PayPal to submit your payment. Your registration will not be complete until BOTH have been done.

Students can save $30 off registration through membership with the American Society for Naturalists. (A net savings of ~$8.50 after accounting for membership fees).

  1. Registration Form
  2. Registration Payment. For Canadians, if you have access to the UBC Speedchart, please contact Mike Whitlock (whitlock@zoology.ubc.ca) to arrange payment.
