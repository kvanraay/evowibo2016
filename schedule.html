Schedule

A welcome reception will take place Friday evening April 15th. The meeting will run from Saturday morning the 16th through midday Sunday the 17th.

Talks will be 15 minutes in length, including the question period. There will be only a single session at a time, in the Commons at Fort Worden.

We'll have two poster sessions on Saturday, each over an hour long, that overlap with the coffee breaks. The coffee will be set up in the same room as the posters, to draw everyone in. By preference, posters will be set up Friday night upon arrival.

A rough, preliminary schedule is as follows:

Friday April 15th

    5:00-10:00 Registration and reception

Saturday April 16th

    7:30-8:30 Breakfast
    8:30 Welcome and introduction
    8:45-10:00 Talk session 1
    10:00-11:15 Poster session 1 and coffee
    11:15-12:30 Talk session 2
    12:30-2:00 Lunch
    2:00-3:30 Talk session 3
    3:30-4:30 Poster session 2 and coffee
    4:30-6:00 Talk session 4
    6:30 Dinner
    7:00 Awards and Town Hall meeting
    8:00-11:00 Social/Mixer

Sunday April 17th

    8:00-9:00 Breakfast
    9:00-10:00 Plenary lecture
    10:00-10:45 Coffee break
    10:45-12:30 Talk session 5
