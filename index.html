EVO-WIBO 2016

Welcome to GitHub Pages.

EVO-WIBO is a gathering of evolutionary biologists of the Pacific Northwest. This meeting is held every second year and typically attracts 120-140 researchers for a fun weekend of presentations and discussions. The 2016 Meeting will be held April 15th-17th, 2016.

As it has from its inception, the conference will be held at in beautiful Port Townsend, WA. This Washington state park is right on Puget Sound on the Olympic Peninsula, with a small marine science center, a nice beach, and many recreation opportunities nearby.
